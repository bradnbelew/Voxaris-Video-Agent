Technology

Built on best-in-class AI infrastructure.

Voxaris AI stacks four production-grade platforms — a photorealistic video replica layer, real-time emotional perception, natural-voice TTS, and a reasoning LLM — so every conversation feels human and every interaction is recorded for recruiter review.

Video Replica
Phoenix-4
Photorealistic conversational video avatars with full facial emotion control and micro-expressions in real time.
Perception
Raven-1
Multimodal perception layer that reads candidate engagement, hesitation, and background context during every session.
Voice
Cartesia sonic-3
Ultra-low-latency TTS with dynamic SSML tagging for per-phrase emotional emphasis and natural delivery.
Turn-Taking
Sparrow-1
Conversational flow layer with high-patience turn detection tuned specifically for interviews, not customer support.
